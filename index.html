About

Building Excellence

Build World Constructions Pvt. Ltd. is the first and only ISO 9001:2015 certified construction company in Kerala, headquartered in Calicut. With a strong presence across Kerala, we have established ourselves as a leading player in the construction industry. Our unwavering commitment to customer service and a dedicated team of professionals has earned us a loyal clientele.

At Build World, we believe in connecting with the outside world and fostering a culture of engagement. Through collaborative efforts and innovative practices, we strive to create a better and more sustainable future. Our strong infrastructure, including a fully functioning employee base, advanced equipment, and transportation capabilities, enables us to deliver projects efficiently and to the highest standards.

Services

Our speciallization

Digital Survey

Our digital survey services involve precise measurements and location marking for construction activities, ensuring accurate positioning, dimensions, and elevation control of structures, ultimately supporting efficient project execution.

Architectural Planning

At Buildworld, we offer comprehensive architectural services, including planning, design, and production, to meet our clients' functional and aesthetic needs. Our expertise extends to pre-design planning, facility programming, site development, entitlement services, and existing conditions surveys.

Structural Engineering

Our skilled structural engineers design buildings that not only possess strength and durability but also showcase architectural beauty and innovative concepts, ensuring lasting structures for generations to come.

Interior Design

Combining creativity with functionality, our interior design services strike a perfect balance between aesthetics and practicality, transforming spaces into personalized havens that reflect your unique style and preferences.

Electrical Design

With a focus on both design integrity and quality installation, our electrical services prioritize factors such as time, quality, and innovation, ensuring the selection of the right contractor based on more than just price.

Mechanical Solutions

Backed by decades of experience in various utilities, our mechanical services encompass installation, development, and estimation, guaranteeing optimal coordination and performance of mechanical components to deliver value and quick returns on investment.

Landscape Design

Our dedicated team of landscape experts brings creativity and cost-effective solutions to your projects, emphasizing innovative design and efficient execution to create stunning outdoor spaces that leave a lasting impression.

Construction Services

Covering all essential aspects of construction contracts, our services include site preparation, building construction, civil engineering, machinery installation, exterior finishing, and related construction services, providing a comprehensive solution for your construction needs.
